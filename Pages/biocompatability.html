Biocompatability Tests

Biocompatability tests may include:
  • Cytotoxicity
  • Sensitization
  • Irritation or Intracutaneous Reactivity
  • Acute Systemic Toxicity
  • Material-Mediated Pyrogenicity
  • Subacute/Subchronic Toxicity
  • Implantation
  • Genotoxicity
  • Chronic Toxicity
  • Hemocompatability
  • Carcinogenicity
Depending on nature of tissue contact and contact duration.
